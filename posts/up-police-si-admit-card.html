UP Police Sub Inspector (SI) Admit Card 2026 Out, Exam City Details

Post Update: Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) ne Sub Inspector aur Platoon Commander posts ke liye Admit Card release kar diye hain. UP Police SI Exam 14-15 March 2026 ko conduct hoga. Niche diye gaye link se apna Admit Card download karein.
Short Details of Notification
Organization Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB)
Post Name Sub Inspector (SI) & Platoon Commander
Official Website uppbpb.gov.in
UP Police SI Exam 2025: Important Dates & Application Fee
Online Apply Start Date 12 August 2025
Online Apply Last Date 11 September 2025
Fee Payment Last Date 15 September 2025
Correction Date 12 - 15 September 2025
Exam Date 14-15 March 2026
Admit Card Available 11 March 2026 (Out Now)
Application Fee (Gen/OBC/EWS) ₹ 500/-
Application Fee (SC/ST) ₹ 400/-
UP Police SI Notification 2025: Age Limits (As On 1 July 2025)
Minimum Age 21 Years
Maximum Age 28 Years
* UP Police ke rules ke mutabik reserved categories ko Age Relaxation ki chhoot milti hai.

Eligibility Details (योग्यता की जानकारी)

  • Umeedwar ke paas kisi bhi recognized University se Graduation (Bachelor’s) degree honi chahiye.
  • Preference: NIELIT se "O" Level Computer Exam pass hone walo ko ya National Cadet Corps (NCC) "B" certificate holders ko preference milegi (Yeh lazmi nahi hai).

UP Police SI Admit Card Kaise Download Karein?

  • UP Police Recruitment Board (UPPRPB) ki official website par visit karein.
  • Homepage par "Latest Updates / Notifications" section open karein ya nicha diye gaye Download Admit Card ke link par click karein.
  • Apna Registration Number ya Enrollment Number, Date of Birth aur Captcha enter karke login karein.
  • Aapka Admit Card screen par display ho jayega. Ise Download karein aur apne exam center par le jaane ke liye printout nikal lein.
