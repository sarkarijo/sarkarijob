How To Check & Download Bihar Police BPSSC SI Pre Result 2026
  • Open Your Internet Browser On Your Mobile Phone Or Computer.
  • Visit The Official Website Of The Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission.
  • On The Homepage, Go To The "Results / Notices" Section.
  • Look For The Link Titled "BPSSC SI Preliminary Result 2026".
  • Click On The Result Notification Link.
  • The Result PDF Will Open On The Screen.
  • Press Ctrl + F And Enter Your Roll Number Or Name To Search For Your Result.
  • If Your Roll Number Appears In The List, It Means You Have Qualified For The Next Stage.
  • Download The Result PDF For Future Reference.
  • Take A Printout Of The Result For Further Recruitment Procedures.
